LETTER TO OBAMA

The Egyptian politician Ayman Nour faces torture since 2005 at the notorious Tora prison, from which he writes a letter of grievance to Senator Barack Obama in June 2008. The following year Obama enters the White House. Four weeks later, in an unexpected turn of events, Nour is suddenly released from Tora prison.
